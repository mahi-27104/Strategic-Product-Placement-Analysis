About the Project

Strategic Product Placement Analysis helps retailers understand how product placement, promotions, pricing, seasonality, and customer demographics affect sales performance.

Using Tableau dashboards and interactive stories, the project uncovers valuable business insights to improve product visibility and maximize revenue.
